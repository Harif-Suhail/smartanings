Overview

When an architectural solution is the right route

This type of project is usually driven by the site. You may be working with a large terrace, an awkward layout, limited fixing points, or a design brief that needs a cleaner finish. In commercial settings, it can also be about creating a consistent look across a bigger outdoor area.

If your requirement is simpler and you want a proven system quickly, these pages are a good place to start: Retractable roof systems, Bioclimatic louvre pergolas, Glass rooms, Awnings.

Custom Fit

From concept to specification

We start with a site survey and a clear conversation about what the space needs to do. Then we look at structure, exposure, fixing options and how the area will be used, before recommending the right approach. This might involve adapting an existing system, combining products, or specifying a structure that suits the site.

If planning is likely, we can provide guidance and we recommend checking with your local council before committing to larger installations.

Setup

A complete outdoor setup, not just a roof

Architectural projects often work best when the roof is planned alongside the rest of the space. Screens, shelter and layout choices can make the area more comfortable and more usable.

For wind control and privacy, see Terrace screens. For flexible coverage on open areas, Giant umbrellas can be a smart addition. If you want lighter style shading for certain zones, Sail shade solutions are worth considering.

For homes with complex requirements

Some homes need a more bespoke solution due to layout, aesthetics or exposure. We can help you achieve a cleaner finish, better coverage, and a setup that suits the property.

If you want a simpler route with a premium result, Bioclimatic louvre pergolas and Glass rooms are popular choices.

For hospitality, venues and commercial spaces

Commercial outdoor areas need to feel dependable. We help create structures that protect covers, support guest comfort, and present well from the first impression.

If you are planning a larger seating area, it may be helpful to combine a main structure with Pagodas and canopies for additional covered zones.
