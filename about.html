Services

What we do

We help customers create outdoor spaces that feel more comfortable and more usable. That can be instant shade from an Awning, a structured solution like Bioclimatic louvre pergolas, flexible all-weather cover with Retractable roof systems, or a bright sheltered area with Glass rooms.

If you are not sure which direction to take, we’ll guide you through the options and recommend what will work best for your space and budget.

Process

How we work

We keep the process clear and straightforward. We start with a conversation to understand what you want the space to do, then we carry out a site survey to check layout, exposure and fixing points. From there, we specify the right system and install it to a high standard so it performs properly and looks right against the property.

If planning may apply, we can provide guidance and we recommend checking with your local council before committing to larger structures.

Applications

Built for homes and commercial spaces

For homeowners, we make patios and gardens easier to use and more comfortable. For hospitality and venues, we help create outdoor areas that feel dependable so guests are happy to stay outside longer.

Many spaces benefit from finishing options that improve comfort. Terrace screens add wind protection and privacy. For flexible coverage on larger areas, Giant umbrellas and Pagodas and canopies can be a strong fit. For a lighter modern look, Shade sails are a popular choice.
