Overview

What an awning does best

An awning is a simple, effective way to control sun and light just outside your doors or frontage. It helps reduce glare, keeps the area cooler, and makes outdoor seating feel more usable. It is also one of the quickest ways to change how a patio or shopfront feels without adding a full structure.

If you need a larger, more enclosed setup, see Retractable roof systems or Glass rooms. If you want a structured roof with airflow control, see Bioclimatic louvre pergolas.

Options

Make the space feel finished

Many projects benefit from one or two additions that improve comfort. Terrace screens help with wind control and privacy, particularly on exposed seating areas. If you want a lightweight shade solution in a different style, Sail shade solutions can be a good fit for certain spaces.

For homes

Awnings make patios easier to use in strong sun and help create a comfortable spot for outdoor dining and relaxing. They can also reduce glare through windows and doors, which often makes indoor spaces feel more comfortable too.

If you want extra privacy and wind protection, add Terrace screens.

For commercial spaces

For cafés, restaurants, pubs and venues, an awning helps protect covers and gives customers a more comfortable place to sit. It can also improve the look of the frontage and help define an outdoor seating area.

If you need wider cover for larger terraces, explore Retractable roof systems. For open areas where you want flexible coverage, see Giant umbrellas or Pagodas and canopies.
