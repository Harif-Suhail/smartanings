Options

Where screens work best

Screens are ideal for patios, balconies, roof terraces and outdoor seating areas that catch wind or feel too open. They work well alongside dining areas, hot tubs, lounge seating, and commercial terraces where you want guests to feel sheltered and settled.

If your main goal is overhead shade, visit Awnings or Sail shade solutions.

For homes

Terrace screens make outdoor areas feel more private and more comfortable. They are a great addition if you want to block a prevailing wind, create separation from neighbouring spaces, or make a patio feel more relaxed for everyday use.

If you are building a larger outdoor setup, combine screens with a Retractable roof system or a Bioclimatic louvre pergola.

For hospitality and commercial terraces

Guests stay longer when they feel comfortable. Screens help reduce draughts and create a more sheltered seating area, which can protect covers and improve the overall experience. They also help define zones on larger terraces, giving the space a more premium layout.

For wider coverage over bigger areas, explore Pagodas and canopies or Giant umbrellas.
