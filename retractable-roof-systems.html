Overview

What a retractable roof system does for your space

A retractable roof gives you control. You can keep the area open in good weather, then close it quickly when conditions change. It’s a practical way to extend how often the space gets used, without committing to a fixed roof all year round.

If you want a more permanent structure, you may prefer a Bioclimatic louvre pergola or a Glass room.

Designed around the site, not forced to fit

Options

Options that change how the space feels

A retractable roof can be set up to suit different spaces and requirements. The right combination of roof style, structure and add-ons makes the difference between basic cover and a space that feels finished.

For extra comfort and control, many customers choose to add side protection. Terrace screens are a popular option for wind control and privacy, especially on exposed areas. If you want a brighter, more enclosed feel, a Glass room may be a better fit.

For homes

A retractable roof gives you shade for warmer days and shelter for those times you still want to sit outside when the weather is unpredictable. It also helps keep the space cooler and more comfortable, especially when the sun hits the patio for long periods.

If your main goal is shade right outside your doors, an Awning could be the simplest solution.

For hospitality and commercial terraces

Outdoor seating works best when it feels dependable. A retractable roof helps protect covers and keeps guests comfortable so the area stays usable for longer. It’s a strong option for restaurants, pubs, hotels and venues where flexibility matters.

For large open areas where you want flexible coverage without a permanent structure, explore Giant umbrellas or Pagodas and canopies.

Questions

FAQs

Can the roof be open and closed quickly?

Yes. Retractable systems are designed to open and close smoothly so you can respond to changing weather without hassle.

Do you install across the UK?

Yes. We are based in the South West and install nationwide.

Do I need planning permission?

It depends on the size, structure and location. We can provide guidance and recommend checking with your local council for larger installations.

Can I add side protection?

Yes. Many setups are enhanced with side protection for wind, privacy and comfort. Terrace screens are a popular choice.

What is the best alternative if I want a fixed roof?

If you want a fixed roof feel with airflow control, see Bioclimatic louvre pergolas. For a brighter enclosed option, see Glass rooms.
