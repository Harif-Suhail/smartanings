Overview

Why giant umbrellas are so effective

A large umbrella gives instant control over sun and light, and it helps create a more comfortable space for guests, customers or family. They are a smart choice when you want coverage over tables or seating zones, but you do not need a permanent roof across the whole area.

If you want a larger fixed installation, see Pagodas and canopies. If you want a system that opens and closes over a bigger footprint, see Retractable roof systems.

Options

Best for terraces, hospitality and open areas

Giant umbrellas work especially well for cafés, restaurants, pubs, hotels and venues. They help protect covers, define seating zones, and give the outdoor space a more premium feel.

For exposed sites, adding side protection can make the area feel calmer. Terrace screens are a popular option for wind reduction and privacy.

Questions

FAQs

Do you install across the UK?

Yes. We are based in the South West and install nationwide.

Are giant umbrellas suitable for commercial use?

Yes. They are widely used for hospitality terraces and venue spaces where flexible coverage is important.

Can umbrellas be used alongside other systems?

Yes. They work well with Pagodas and canopies for larger sites, and Terrace screens for wind protection and comfort.

What if I need a more permanent covered area?

Retractable roof systems, bioclimatic louvre pergolas, and glass rooms are good alternatives depending on how enclosed you want the space to feel.
