Step 1

Design and survey

A successful installation starts with a proper site survey. We assess measurements, fixing points, exposure to wind and sun, and how you want to use the space. Then we recommend the most suitable option from our range and confirm the right specification for your property.

If you are comparing options, these pages are a good place to start: Retractable roof systems, Bioclimatic louvre pergolas, Awnings, Glass rooms.

Step 2

Installation

We install our systems to maintain quality and ensure everything performs as it should. That includes correct fixings, tidy finishing, and a clear handover so you know how to use and care for your product.

If you want to complete the space, we can also supply add-ons like Terrace screens for wind protection and privacy.
